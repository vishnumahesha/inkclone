Train InkClone on your handwriting in three steps.

1
Print & fill the template

Download and print the handwriting template, then fill in every cell with the corresponding letter or character.

  • Write naturally — don't try to be neater than usual
  • Use any dark pen — black ink works best
  • Fill every cell; leave none blank
  • One character per box, sized to fit
Tip: Write the template in the same pen you use day-to-day for the most authentic output.
2
Photograph the filled template

Take a clear photo of each completed page.

  • Keep all four corner marks visible — they anchor the grid alignment
  • Shoot straight above the page; tilts up to ~10° are auto-corrected
  • Even lighting — avoid shadows across the page
  • Any resolution works; full-resolution is fine but 800px+ is sufficient
  • PNG or JPEG both accepted; lossless preferred
Use a black ballpoint pen. Pencil and light-coloured pens can fade past the extraction threshold and produce blank glyphs.
3
Upload and process

Upload a photo of each template page. InkClone will extract your glyphs automatically.
